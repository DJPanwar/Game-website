Ninjas

Objective: Use the Draw button to draw bridges, guiding the ninja through levels.

Gameplay: Click and drag to create sturdy lines, ensuring the ninja safely crosses.

Challenge: Mastering precise drawing for efficient navigation.

Snake

In the snake game, we use four buttons for up, down, left, and right. There is a red dot to eat, which increases the length of the snake.

Tik Tok Tac

In the "Tik Tok Tac" game, players take turns placing their symbols (X or O) on a 3x3 grid. The goal is to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of three of their symbols to win the game.

Connect 4

In Connect 4, two players take turns dropping colored discs into a vertical grid. The objective is to connect four of their discs in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally before the opponent does, leading to victory.

Simon

In Simon, replicate a sequence of lights and sounds by pressing colored buttons, testing memory. Advance by accurately repeating the pattern to score in this classic memory game.

Card Memory Match

Setup: Shuffle and lay out cards face down in a grid.

Gameplay: Turn over two cards per turn, aiming to find matching pairs. Continue until all pairs are matched.

Winning: The player with the most pairs when all cards are matched is the winner.
